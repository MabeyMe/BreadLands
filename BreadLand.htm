Server Rules

A few simple rules to keep things fun for everyone.

  • ✔ Be Respectful: No harassment, griefing, or hate speech. Treat all players with kindness.
  • ✔ No Cheating: Using hacked clients, x-ray, or exploits is strictly forbidden.
  • ✔ No Stealing or Griefing: Don't take items from other players' chests or destroy their builds.
  • ✔ Build Responsibly: Avoid leaving floating trees or creating lag-inducing machines. Keep the server beautiful.
  • ✔ Have Fun: That's why we're all here! Enjoy your time and make some new friends.
